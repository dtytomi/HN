HNC Market Research

As a guiding principle the outcomes, strategies and insights that a research /business objective seeks to achieve forms our starting point and POV from the inception of a project. This enables us to see and execute research in a different way. It is thus a wasteful activity if the research will not be used or lead the client to a better future.

Thus in thinking, we:

Engage
the client to further understand their briefs, objectives and outcomes
Execute
the research activities with the outmost professionalism, integrity, transparency, speed and affordable costing.
Explain
the insight from the research with real time business/organizational reality in order to build or develop a strong brand

Our area of capabilities within market research includes:

Consulting


The consulting arm of HNC ensures that clients get suitable strategies, advice and relevant information that will help in the promotion of their goods or services. Our strength covers different areas ranging from market entry advisory to issues regarding corporate reputation of organizations. Business development is rendered as well as loyalty customer experience and satisfaction which measures the interactions and experiences of consumers or customers with the services or products of our clients in addition is the employee experience and satisfaction not leaving out governance matrices as it applies to the clients.

Marketing

The broad range of our marketing competencies includes advertising research which involves gathering and analyzing relevant information that will help our clients track effectiveness of adverts and communication with customers. In addition we handle quick surveys, field and data tabulation, brand health of organizations, ideas or concept testing, product testing to help clients measure their performance and possible acceptance of new products especially in a very competitive market, as well as mystery surveillance which cutting across various sectors of the economy. A method we use for monitoring or measuring dealings between a consumer and our clients or assess the quality of a product or service.

Retail Audit


At HNC, we see the need for our clients to have important insight as regards their sales volumes and trends, effectiveness and other relevant features within the competitive market. Our retail audit involves trade assessment and retail activation which has helped clients discover inconsistences and their actual place in the market thereby making informed decisions to improve and grow.

Trainings


As part of our capacity building service, we organize, facilitate and present workshops, seminars and conferences that will further cause improvement and growth for business of our clients or give further insights into issues that require depth and better understanding in making strategic decisions that will enhance productivity or services and upgrade outputs.
