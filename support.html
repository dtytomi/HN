Operation Support

The HNOS is a newly created support system for researchers or organizations seeking for project managers to support their activities in planning, coordinating, budgeting, reporting their research activities. This helps the client evaluate and estimate the needed resource necessary to complete various activities per time. It also helps NGOs, Donor funded researchers or low budget researchers to leverage on the pool of experience in the area of research using tools or software to assist their analyses and interprete research findings in a way that will answer the research questions and objectives.

We assist to script, host, deploy and arrange those data in a readable format (SPSS, EXCEL, CSV rules, Transcripts etc) which are then analyzed. We provide support in the area of interpreting secondary and advance analyses should the need arise in addition to training, support, field assistance and sampling, data hosting, analyses and insight sessions.
