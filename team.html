Our Project Management Team

  1. Home
  2. Our Project Management Team

Consist of individual with expertise in the following areas:

Client Servicing


  • We exhibit clear understanding of client business, operations and strategies and how HN Conseil will help you achieve it
  • We begin every process with interaction and planning with the client’s needs in focus
  • We also work with a structured coordination and highly technical skills among team members to deliver on every client requests

RFQ/RFPs/Proposal Process

  • Explore and discuss the brief with client
  • Gather and check cost quotations against specification
  • Responsible for project design, management and execution
  • Undertake specific project related functions including writing proposals, designing questionnaires/discussion guides, sample design and tab specs.

Cost and Finance


  • Our desire is that every study is important and seeks to address a need. We always propose cost saving solutions to ensure such study materializes and addresses clients objectives
  • Our GM and profit on projects are reasonable to allow for the sustainability of the business and that which the client can afford
  • We are prudent and highlight costs and budget concerns/options to achieve reasonable and affordable clients budget without compromising on quality
  • We liaise with credible field agencies to secure best project estimates/budgets as well as coordinate the project

Project Management


  • Approve itinerary provided by Operations department (Field & DP)
  • Brief the Quality Control, Data Management, Field and Project Management team
  • Develop project related materials – colour card, concept cards, showcards, memory aid cards, picture cards, training and briefing manuals, DP specs, code frames etc
  • Effectively communicate project needs to vendors
  • Ensure implementation of the best and fastest project schedules and processes
  • Key account management for liaison with vendors
  • Responsible for orderly & effective process and quality documentation
  • Update Account Managers on progress and problems as and when they occur on live projects

Analysis/reporting


  • Provide analyses & research findings to meet client needs
  • Check, confirm and approve data before commencement of charting
  • Generate actionable insights and recommendations to meet client needs
  • Collect secondary information from sources such as website, social platforms, prints, press and industry reports to back up inputs in the analysis
  • Responsible for timely and quality deliverables

Team and People Management


  • Setup a clear directions, objectives and questions where appropriate
  • Promote team bonding, engagement and spirit
  • Supervise, Coordinate, Control, Manage, Monitor and Maintain standards throughout the lifecycle of each project

Communication


  • Liaise with client /relevant internal departments on project related matters
  • Prepare presentations using HNC’s tools and specific software
  • Seek approval where necessary and communicate any changes
  • Share periodic update on project life stages
